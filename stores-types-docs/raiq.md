## 4. Typography (Font Strategy)

* **Primary Font (Arabic):** A custom Kufi-inspired modern font with clean, non-decorative lines.
* **Secondary Font (English):** A high-end Sans-Serif (e.g., *Montserrat Light* or *Futura*) for descriptions and "Kitchen & Home" taglines.
* **Hierarchy:** Bold headers for product names, Light/Regular weight for body text.

---

## 5. Product & Packaging Specs

Since you sell physical goods, the branding must translate well to physical touchpoints:

* **Materiality:** Preference for matte finishes, recycled cardboard, and laser-engraved wood/metal.
* **Packaging Concept:** "The Unboxing Serenity." Minimal text on the outside, with the "Raiq" logo centered.
* **Photography Style:** Soft, natural lighting (Golden hour) with plenty of negative space (white/neutral backgrounds).
